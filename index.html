Built on seventeen years in the fertilizer trade

AR Traders was founded in January 2024 to do one thing well: dependable fertilizer distribution for the dealers and farms of Punjab.

Our foundation is deep sector experience. Before establishing AR Traders, our founder spent seventeen years with Engro Fertilizers — one of Pakistan's leading fertilizer manufacturers — working across sales, distribution, and pricing. That background shapes how we operate today: disciplined credit management, transparent pricing, and genuine agronomic understanding of what each crop and soil needs.

From our head office in Lahore and branch offices in Kasur, Gujranwala, Layyah, and Rajanpur, we serve dealers across central and southern Punjab with a full range of nitrogen, phosphate, and potash fertilizers alongside micronutrients.

Expanding into organic & bio-based inputs

Pakistan's soils need more than NPK. AR Traders is actively developing its organic and bio-based portfolio — compost, organic fertilizers, and biostimulants — and building partnerships with international producers to bring proven soil-health products to Pakistani farms. We participate in international trade platforms, including Ecomondo (Rimini, Italy), to source technologies and supply partners for this growing segment.
