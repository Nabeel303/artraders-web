Understanding Phosphate

Building Strong Foundations for Crop Development

Phosphate is one of the essential primary nutrients required throughout the early stages of crop growth.

It supports healthy root development, encourages strong crop establishment, contributes to flowering, and plays an important role in plant energy transfer.

Balanced phosphate nutrition helps crops develop a strong foundation for healthy growth throughout the season.

Strong crops begin with strong roots.
