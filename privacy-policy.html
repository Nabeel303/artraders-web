Privacy Policy

How AR Traders collects, uses, safeguards, and manages information provided through our corporate website.

Effective date

1. Introduction

AR Traders (“AR Traders,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects the privacy of visitors to www.artraders.net.pk. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect through the website, how we use it, when it may be shared, and the choices available to website visitors.

By using this website or submitting information through it, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us, request information, or submit a business enquiry. This may include:

  • Your name and job title
  • Company or organization name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Country, city, or business location
  • Product interests and enquiry details
  • Any other information included in your message

We may also receive limited technical information automatically when you visit the website, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, pages viewed, and approximate access time.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information collected through the website to:

  • Respond to enquiries, requests, and business communications
  • Provide information about products, distribution, sourcing, or partnership opportunities
  • Evaluate potential customers, suppliers, manufacturers, distributors, or business partners
  • Operate, secure, maintain, and improve the website
  • Understand website traffic and user interaction
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, or security incidents
  • Meet applicable legal, regulatory, accounting, or compliance requirements

4. Legal and Business Basis for Processing

We process information where it is reasonably necessary to respond to your request, pursue legitimate business interests, perform or prepare for a business relationship, protect our systems and rights, or comply with applicable obligations.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website may use cookies or similar technologies for essential functionality, security, traffic analysis, and performance measurement. Cookies are small files placed on a device by a website.

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how some website features operate.

6. Analytics, Maps, Hosting, and Other Service Providers

We may use third-party service providers for website hosting, analytics, security, maps, content delivery, email handling, or related technical functions. These providers may process limited information on our behalf and may operate under their own privacy terms.

Where third-party tools are used, their collection and use of information are governed by their respective policies and service terms.

7. How We Share Information

AR Traders does not sell or rent personal information. We may share information only where reasonably necessary:

  • With service providers supporting website or business operations
  • With professional advisers, auditors, or consultants
  • With affiliated business parties where required to evaluate or fulfil a legitimate enquiry
  • To comply with law, regulation, court order, or lawful governmental request
  • To protect the rights, property, security, or legitimate interests of AR Traders or others
  • In connection with a business restructuring, transfer, merger, or similar transaction

8. International Communications and Transfers

AR Traders works with customers, suppliers, manufacturers, and potential partners in Pakistan and other countries. Information submitted through the website may therefore be communicated to or processed in another jurisdiction when reasonably necessary for a legitimate business purpose.

9. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including follow-up on business enquiries, recordkeeping, dispute resolution, security, and legal or compliance requirements.

10. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no website, internet transmission, or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Your Choices and Requests

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have submitted through the website, subject to identity verification and any applicable legal, contractual, or recordkeeping requirements.

You may also ask us to stop using your contact details for non-essential business communications.

12. Third-Party Links

The website may contain links to third-party websites or services. AR Traders is not responsible for the content, security, availability, or privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage visitors to review the relevant third-party policies before providing information.

13. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for business and professional audiences. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children through the website.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our website, business practices, technology, or applicable requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
