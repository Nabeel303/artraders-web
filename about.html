Our Story

Built on Experience. Focused on the Future.

AR Traders was established in March 2023 with a clear purpose: to build a dependable agricultural inputs business grounded in industry knowledge, trusted relationships, and professional execution.

The company was founded by a leader with more than seventeen years of experience in Pakistan's fertilizer industry, bringing practical expertise across sales, distribution, pricing strategy, dealer development, supply chain management, and market intelligence.

What began as a fertilizer distribution business has developed into a broader Agricultural Inputs & Supply Chain Company serving Punjab's agricultural markets while expanding into international sourcing, specialty crop nutrition, and sustainable agricultural solutions.

Our journey is guided by one principle: create lasting value for agriculture through trust, reliability, and responsible growth.

Who We Are

AR Traders operates at the intersection of agriculture, distribution, supply chain management, and international trade.

We deliver agricultural inputs through a professionally coordinated network that connects manufacturers and suppliers with dealers, distributors, and farming communities. Our role extends beyond product movement—we create dependable market access, strengthen business relationships, and support long-term growth across the agricultural value chain.
